Samantha DiMauro

A native New Englander, I moved to Boston to study at Emerson College, and naturally never left. Why would I? Boston has it all—from scholars to coffee houses to countless important sights in American history. Plus, the Red Sox! When I’m not writing for Panorama: The Official Guide to Boston, you can find me lounging in the Public Garden, on the hunt for vintage finds, or trying the strangest cocktail on the menu.
